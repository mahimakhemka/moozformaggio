THE MOOZ STORY

We, at Mooz, make sure to manufacture the best artisan Italian cheese made of the best milk freshly collected from the farms of Haryana. Our strict norms and policies ensure our product quality and in turn our customer's full satisfaction. Crafted by an Italian specialist fourth generation cheese-maker, we promise to deliver you nothing but best in every Mooz product. Pick out your favourites from our selection of in-house cheese like Mascarpone, Buffolo Fresca, Ricotta and many more. Our full range of European cheese is put togerther with the finest Indian Buffallo, Cow and Goat's milk.
Mooz manufactures and supplies products, shares many recipes across its social media and holds a number of different events.
